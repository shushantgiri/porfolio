Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?
I am currently based in Kathmandu, Nepal, but I am available for travel projects too!
Do you still capture photos?
Yes, I capture random portrait photos and enjoy street photography and other spontaneous moments during my travels.
What types of photos do you click?
I enjoy capturing portraits, street photography, and random moments that I find interesting during my travels.
Can I book you for a private photoshoot?
I do not offer private photoshoots, as I prefer to capture spontaneous moments and enjoy photography on my own terms.
How much do you charge per session?
I do not charge for photoshoots. I capture images purely for the joy of photography.
Do you offer photo editing services?
I prefer to keep my photos as natural as possible, but I may make basic edits for personal projects.
