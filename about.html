The Story of My Growth

Hey, I'm Shushant Giri, the person behind Shushant Photography. I'm 18 years old, living in Lalitpur, originally from Dang Deukhuri. Let me share a bit about my journey. Life has been a mix of adventures, starting with making gaming videos in 7th grade and then trying out blogging after finishing 10th grade. Each experience has shaped my story, and I'm excited to tell you more about it. The journey has had its challenges, including the ups and downs of gaming videos and blogging. However, these experiences taught me a lot and made me determined to keep going. For the last two years, I've been deeply passionate about photography. Learning and improving my skills has been incredibly enjoyable, and I've developed a strong passion for capturing memorable moments. At the same time, I've been exploring coding—learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I even created my own website! After completing my 12th-grade exams, I used that free time to learn UI/UX design. Now, I'm a designer as well and focus on user experience design. It's been a bit tricky but also really rewarding. A big thanks to my support system, especially my Mama, Madhav Prasad Belbase . His guidance has been invaluable on this journey. Thank you for reading about my journey! Your support means a lot to me, and I'm excited to share more of my story with you in the future!

Experience

I have nearly three years of experience in photography, capturing and creating memorable images of various events. I also have one year of experience as a UI/UX designer at Jyrasoft Technologies, where I design user-friendly digital experiences.
